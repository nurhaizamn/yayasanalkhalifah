Activities of YAK



Yayasan Al-Khalifah 'Ihsan Ramadhan Covid 19' Humanitarian Aid Mission in the interior of Pahang


  • Through this activity, we has found with 65 Orang Asli Muslim convert families at Pos Lenjang.

  • Every village in Pos Lenjang which is Kg. Nangka, Kg Rakoh, Kg Dayok, Kg Sop, Kg Lenjang and Kg Kenderong we have come for distribution matters.

  • The donation given consists of rice and kitchen items amounting to RM50 as well as a donation from VAsia which is raisin chocolate.

  • Thanks be to God, these inland relatives of the natives received sustenance from benefactors during the MCO period that was implemented until June 9, 2020.


Mission in Helping 20 New Orang Asli Convert to Islam
