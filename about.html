Background of Yayasan Al-Khalifah

Yayasan Al-Khalifah (YAK) is a non-profit organisation (NGO) founded on November 16, 2006 and registered with the company commission as a 'Limited by Guarantee' business. The major objective of YAK is the 'Indigenous People' target group under the Indigenous Peoples' Development and Preaching Programme Unit (PPDOA).

The organisation, however, also assists other communities through funding initiatives for Orphans and the Poor, New Brothers, Rohingya, Foreign and Domestic Humanitarian Aid.
