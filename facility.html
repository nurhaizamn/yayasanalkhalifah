MASJID AR-RAHMAN

This mosque was ready to be established in 2018 in Pulau Gajah, Kota Bahru. Every corner of this mosque is highlighted with the beauty and delicacy of Malay architecture. This decorative art is the result of a combination of 4 countries namely Indonesia, Turkey, Morocco and Yemen. This has made it one of the unique mosques in Malaysia.
